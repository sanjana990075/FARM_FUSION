Farm Fusion – A Compact, Multi-Purpose Machine for Small Scale Farmers

Farm Fusion is a low-cost, multi-power agricultural machine designed to support small-scale farmers with Harvesting, Spraying, and Fertiliser Management using a single, modular platform.

About the Project

Why Farm Fusion?

Decline of Small Farmers

Small-scale farmers are decreasing by nearly 3–4% every year due to rising costs and low returns.

All-in-One Machine

Farm Fusion combines soil prep, sowing, spraying, and harvesting into a single compact machine.

Reduces Dependency

Eliminates the need for buying or renting multiple heavy machines for basic farming activities.

AgriLink App

Our app helps farmers sell crops directly to customers, removing middlemen and increasing profit.

Support Centers

We plan to set up 2–3 village service centers for machine maintenance and farmer support.

6-Month Free Trial

Farmers get 6 months of Farm Fusion to build trust and encourage adoption.

Project Journey

From Idea to Prototype

Problem Identification

Observed challenges faced by small farmers in accessing large, costly machinery for basic farm tasks.

Concept & Design

Designed a compact machine with modular attachments and multi-power support to keep it practical and affordable.

Prototype Development

Developed a working prototype with detachable tools, storage compartments, and adjustable spray pipes.

Testing & Refinement

Iterated on the base frame, wheel rotation and tool mounting with feedback from mentors and vendors.
